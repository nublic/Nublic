Your domestic cloud

Nublic is fully compatible with televisions, media centers, sound equipments and much more. Just plug your Nublic to your router and every device in your local network will be able to access all the media stored in Nublic.

With all the security deserved to manage your data. Only in your local net and with the posibility to hide any file that needs more privacy.

(Digital Living Network Alliance) is a protocol for the sharing of digital media between devices such as computers, printers, cameras, cell phones, and other multimedia devices. With Nublic you will have a DLNA server which provides all your centralized data to the periferic multimedia devices.

Access from everywhere

Even when you are not at home Nublic offers you access to all your data. With our user friendly web interfaces you will be able to play your music, view your pictures, stream your videos, access your documents and much more from anywhere. Take a look at the Nublic web applications included in the device!

Use your own personal, private URL, your username and your password to access all the data stored in your home and all the services offered by your Nublic device. Confortable, secure and more private than ever on the internet, from all over the world.
