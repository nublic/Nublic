Your domestic cloud

Nublic is fully compatible with televisions, media centers, sound equipments and much more. Just plug your Nublic to your router and every device in your local network will be able to access all the media stored in Nublic.

With all the security deserved to manage your data. Only in your local net and with the posibility to hide any file that needs more privacy.

Nublic is a DLNA server which provides all your centralized data to the multimedia devices at home.

Access from everywhere

Use your own personal URL, your username and your password to access all the data stored in your home and all the services offered by your Nublic applications. Confortable, secure and more private than ever on the internet.

Access to Nublic using your favourite web browser, such as Google Chrome , Firefox , Safari , Opera or Internet Explorer .

Access using a Secure FTP client to your files in Nublic.

In sync with your computer

Nublic allows you to access your data always in your own terms. Use Windows , Mac or Linux to manage your information in Nublic.

With SAMBA you will access Nublic from your OS browser in a native way. For instance, you will find Nublic folders on "My network sites" on Windows.

Sparkleshare is a software that allows you to synchronize the local files in your computer with Nublic automatically. Surprisingly effortless backup of any file in your Nublic.

Mobile phone friendly

You will have at your disposal mobile specific applications to access to all the services that Nublic provides you from your portable device.

Download the Nublic application in your Android phone or tablet and start enjoying all the Nublic power all over the world.

You will find the Nublic application for iOS in Apple Store to delight Nublic in your iPhone or iPad.
