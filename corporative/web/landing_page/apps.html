Files

Browse your files fast and easy from any computer or table. With the feeling of a local file browser but over the net. Have all your files accesible from everywhere with Nublic.

Easy navigation to find, view, organize and manage your files and folders. Everything is designed to be straightforward, comfortable and quick.

And you will be able to view all your content without the need of installing any additional software. The easiest and fastest way to access to your files.

Nublic supports almost every format. To give an idea of that fact we will list some of them, but there are many more supported! That includes pdf, doc, odt, rtf, txt, mp3, wav, mpeg, avi, jpg, gif, png, ...

Music

Thanks to Nublic your music is accessible in your local network via DLNA, but this application grants you access everywhere via a web browser.

You can enjoy a high quality music player which streams your own audio files. Nublic even completes the meta-data that lacks in your music files, so your music will always be completely organized.

Nublic music player will also allow you to create music collections easily. It is prepared to host huge amounts of music and it will always be at your fingertips.

And create playlists too! You can prepare lists of songs to be played in a fixed order, and you will be able to access them everywhere. Have at your disposal your party playlists when you need them!

Photos

With Nublic photos application you can view your pictures everywhere. The interface is very easy to use, which makes it very usefull.

You can find inside this app all the images that you like more from your Nublic. And remember you can always access them wherever you are!

And you can create albums to organize all the pictures. You can maintain your photographs under your control at your own home and, at the same time, share a link to the images you want to show to your friends.

With Nublic your photos will no longer be exposed on internet and social networks.

Bazaar

And this does not end here. With the Nublic bazzar you will always be able to expand your Nublic. Have your Nublic tailor-made for you!

Stay updated with the upcoming apps and get the best of your Nublic device. Add applications whenever you want or need them and discover all the possibilities that Nublic offers you.
